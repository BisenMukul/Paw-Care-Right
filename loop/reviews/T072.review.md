## Carry-forwards
**T073 (RC webhook write path):**
- Unknown `rcAppUserId`: `upsert` FKs to `User.id` (`onDelete: Cascade`) — an event for an
  app_user_id that is not a known user will raise a **FK violation**. Webhook must resolve/skip
  such events, not 500-loop.
- Idempotency: no `eventId` column here by design — T073 must dedupe RC events itself.
- `householdId` stamping: must write the purchaser's **current** household at upsert time
  (drives the family query). Stale `householdId` after a member moves can transiently over-grant
  two households (R2) and self-heals on the next webhook — never a safety issue.
- R6: household deletion cascades the mirror row; row self-heals on next upsert.
**T074 (quota/paywall):**
- Consume `BillingService` (already `exports`ed). MUST NOT premium-gate any §5 safety surface
  (red-flag rules, Emergency interstitial, `<VetDisclaimer/>`, symptom-check safe-fallback).
